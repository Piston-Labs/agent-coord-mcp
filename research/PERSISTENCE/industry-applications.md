## Key Takeaways

1. **Coordination is the moat** - Individual AI capabilities commoditizing; orchestration creates value
2. **Domain expertise matters** - Best AI companies are vertical (Harvey, PathAI, BloombergGPT)
3. **Human-AI hybrid wins** - Pure automation fails; co-pilot modes succeed
4. **Speed vs quality tradeoff** - Fast-moving markets (gaming) vs high-stakes (healthcare)
5. **Legal landscape evolving** - IP, liability, regulation still uncertain
