## Funding Stage Breakdown

### Pre-Seed / Seed ($1-5M)

**Use of Funds:**
- Founding team (3-5 people)
- Initial experiments
- Architecture research
- Data pipeline prototyping

**Typical Investors:**
- Angel investors
- AI-focused pre-seed funds
- University/lab spin-out funding

### Series A ($20-50M)

**Use of Funds:**
- Train first competitive model (7-13B)
- Expand team to 20-30
- Build evaluation infrastructure
- Initial product/API development

**Typical Investors:**
- Tier 1 VCs (a16z, Sequoia, Founders Fund)
- Strategic investors (cloud providers)

### Series B ($100-300M)

**Use of Funds:**
- Train 70B+ competitive model
- Scale team to 50-100
- Production infrastructure
- Commercial go-to-market

**Typical Investors:**
- Growth equity
- Sovereign wealth funds
- Big tech strategic investment

### Series C+ ($500M-5B)

**Use of Funds:**
- Frontier model development
- Massive compute clusters
- Global team expansion
- Multi-product development

**Typical Investors:**
- Microsoft, Google, Amazon
- SoftBank, Tiger Global
- Sovereign wealth funds

## Funding Pitch Elements

### What Investors Want to See

1. **Team** - World-class ML researchers (ex-Google Brain, OpenAI, DeepMind)
2. **Technical edge** - Novel architecture or training approach
3. **Data advantage** - Unique or proprietary data sources
4. **Capital efficiency** - More with less compute
5. **Go-to-market** - Clear path to revenue
6. **Safety commitment** - Responsible development

### Key Metrics for AI Startups

| Metric | Seed | Series A | Series B |
|--------|------|----------|----------|
| Team size | 3-10 | 20-40 | 50-100 |
| Benchmark perf | - | Top 10 | Top 5 |
| Revenue | $0 | $1-5M ARR | $10-50M ARR |
| Customers | Pilots | 10-50 | 100+ |

## Financial Projections Template

### Year 1-2 (R&D Phase)
- Revenue: $0-1M (research partnerships)
- Burn: $2-5M/month
- Focus: Model development

### Year 2-3 (Launch Phase)
- Revenue: $5-20M ARR
- Burn: $5-10M/month
- Focus: API launch, first customers

### Year 3-4 (Scale Phase)
- Revenue: $50-200M ARR
- Burn: Approaching breakeven
- Focus: Enterprise, new products

### Year 4-5 (Growth Phase)
- Revenue: $200M-1B ARR
- Profitable operations
- Focus: Market leadership
