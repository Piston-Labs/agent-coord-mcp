# Development Timeline for Building AI

> Realistic phases, milestones, and what to expect at each stage

## Executive Summary

| Phase | Duration | Investment | Outcome |
|-------|----------|------------|---------|
| Foundation | 6-12 months | $5-20M | Base model, team, infrastructure |
| Competitive | 12-18 months | $20-100M | 70B-class model, product-market fit |
| Frontier | 24-36 months | $100M-1B | GPT-4 class, market leader |

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-12)

### Goals
- Assemble core team
- Build training infrastructure
- Train first competitive model (7-13B parameters)
- Establish evaluation frameworks

### Month 1-3: Team & Infrastructure

| Week | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1-2 | Founding team finalized (3-5 people) |
| 3-4 | Cloud/compute contracts signed |
| 5-8 | Data pipeline v1 operational |
| 9-12 | Training infrastructure validated |

**Key Hires:**
- Research Lead
- ML Engineer (Training)
- Infrastructure Engineer
- Data Engineer

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Compute cluster operational (64+ GPUs)
- [ ] Data pipeline processing 1TB/day
- [ ] Training framework validated
- [ ] Evaluation harness set up

### Month 4-6: First Model Training

| Week | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 13-14 | Architecture finalized |
| 15-18 | Pre-training run (7B model) |
| 19-20 | Evaluation and debugging |
| 21-24 | Fine-tuning experiments |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] 7B base model trained
- [ ] Benchmark results (MMLU, HellaSwag, etc.)
- [ ] SFT pipeline working
- [ ] Initial safety evaluations

### Month 7-12: Product Development

| Week | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 25-30 | API development |
| 31-36 | Alpha testing with partners |
| 37-42 | Iterate based on feedback |
| 43-48 | Public beta preparation |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Inference API operational
- [ ] 10+ alpha users testing
- [ ] Cost model validated
- [ ] Safety protocols documented

## Phase 2: Competitive (Months 13-30)

### Goals
- Train 70B-class model
- Achieve product-market fit
- Build commercial traction
- Establish research agenda

### Month 13-18: Scale Up

| Milestone | Target Date |
|-----------|-------------|
| Team expands to 20-30 | Month 13-14 |
| 70B training begins | Month 15 |
| 70B model complete | Month 18 |
| Commercial launch | Month 18-20 |

**Key Hires:**
- Additional researchers (5-10)
- Safety/Alignment team (2-3)
- Product manager
- Go-to-market team

### Month 19-24: Commercial Traction

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| API users | 1,000+ |
| Revenue | $1-5M ARR |
| Enterprise customers | 5-10 |
| Model quality | Top 10 on benchmarks |

### Month 25-30: Differentiation

- Establish unique research direction
- Build proprietary data advantages
- Develop specialized capabilities
- Prepare for Series B/C

## Risk-Adjusted Timeline

### Optimistic Scenario
- Everything goes well
- Strong funding, great team
- Timeline: -20% from estimates

### Realistic Scenario
- Normal challenges
- Some setbacks
- Timeline: As stated above

### Pessimistic Scenario
- Major obstacles
- Talent/funding issues
- Timeline: +50% from estimates

## What Can Go Wrong

| Risk | Impact | Probability |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Training run fails | 2-3 month delay | Medium |
| Key hire leaves | 3-6 month setback | Medium |
| Compute shortage | Significant delay | Low |
| Regulatory block | Could be fatal | Low |
| Funding gap | 6-12 month pause | Medium |
| Safety incident | Reputation damage | Low |
